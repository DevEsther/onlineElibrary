chapter 1

As Sarah walked home from work one evening, she heard the sound of shouting coming from a nearby alleyway. She cautiously approached the source of the noise and saw a group of young men surrounding a teenage girl. The girl was crying and pleading with them to leave her alone, but they seemed intent on harassing her. Sarah's instincts kicked in, and she knew she couldn't just stand by and watch. She walked towards the group of men and firmly but calmly intervened, telling them to leave the girl alone and back off. The men initially ignored her and continued their taunts, but Sarah stood her ground and kept talking to them, using a calm and confident tone. Gradually, the men began to back off, and Sarah escorted the girl out of the alley and made sure she got home safely. The girl thanked Sarah for her intervention, and Sarah felt a sense of pride and satisfaction knowing that she had made a difference in someone's life. From that day on, she made a commitment to herself to always speak up and intervene when she saw someone being mistreated or bullied.
